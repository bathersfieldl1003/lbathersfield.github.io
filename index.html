About Me

Starting in 2003, I was accepted to Bowie State University as a Computer Networking Major. During that time, I joined the BSU Symphony of Soul as a Sousaphone Player on Band Scholarship for three years, learning the instrument without the ability to read music. During my Junior year, I stepped away from BSU to work with my family Caribbean Restaurant to build business and management skills. In 2011, I found my first IT job as a Help Desk Tech for a National Non-Profit for a cure for Cystic Fibrosis. Building those skills, I participated in classes and received my first certification in Cisco(CCNA). While working to find a job in the field, I re-dedicated my time to networking and worked partime to get back into school. Now, I am in my final semester of my senior year and revisiting my HTML skills to complete my final project for CTEC 450 with this portfolio.
